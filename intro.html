Why We Started Teatergent

Be the solution, not the pollution.

Founded in 2017, Teatergent aims to reinvent kitchen detergents and change people’s life one step at a time. Founder Joshua Chao got the inspiration from his own family since he was a kid. His mom replaced chemical detergent by tea-seed powder for dishwashing, simply because it doesn’t cause her hand eczema.

 

Years later, Chao learnt tea-seed powder is actually the byproduct of producing Camellia oil, a high-quality edible plant oil similar to Olive oil. He immediately decided to re-design this food-grade, organic, nontoxic and eco-friendly detergent to make everybody benefits from it. After three years of research and development, the world’s first organic tea-seed kitchen detergent is born, and it’s named Teatergent.

How You Can Use Teatergent

For Multipurpose Cleaning

Dissolve 4 cubes into a 450 mL bottle of water

You can wash utensils, vegetables, fruits, chopping board, baby bottles and pet bowls by Teatergent liquid.

 

It is pH 5.7 weak acid, similar to human body. All natural ingredients without toxic chemicals won’t hurt your skin.

Store Teatergent liquid bottle in the fridge

Without any chemical preservative, Teatergent liquid will slowly get fermented under room temperature. Please store the liquid bottle in the fridge when not in use.

For Dishwasher

Put 1 cube into dishwasher detergent dispenser

It is not only food-grade, organic and toxin-free, but also much more powerful than chemical detergent, leaving the dishes spotless clean without any invisible residues.
