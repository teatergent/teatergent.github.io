Teatergent, an organic detergent made from tea.

Tea is popular beverage.

People love to drink chai tea, black tea, green tea, herbal tea, bubble tea and a lot more tea variants.

 

Tea is popular food.

People in the Eastern world use tea oil to cook, just like people in the Western world use olive oil.

 

But you may not know, tea is also detergent.

Remember how refreshing when you drink tea? That is something called “tea saponin” decomposing oil and grease in your mouth.

 

By extracting and concentrating “tea saponin” by nearly a thousand times, a household detergent is created.

 

We call it Teatergent.

Tea (Camellia sinensis) illustration from Köhler's Medizinal-Pflanzen.

Compressing “tea saponin” powder into solid cubes.

The world’s first detergent produced in food factory.

Extraction from tea.

It requires 40 kilograms of tea to extract the sufficient amount of “tea saponin” to produce 1 cube.

 

Other edible ingredients.

Enzymes from pineapple, papaya and corn are added, in order to optimize the cleaning performance.

 

Food production line.

All these edible ingredients are qualified to process in FDA certified food factory. No artificial chemicals.

 Teatergent cubes produced in FDA certified food factory.
