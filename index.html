Made From Tea Seed

It all starts with growing tea trees in the

mountain, because “tea seed” takes three

quarters of Teatergent ingredients. That’s

unheard-of in the detergent industry.

Our tea trees are organically grown

6 grams of tea

seed powder is

After being sun-

dried, tea seeds

are peeled and

pulverized with

It takes about an

year for the tea

seeds to grow.

When their shells

turn from green to

brown, it means

they are now

mature enough to

be harvested.

Insisting on being ethically and

ecologically responsible every step

it is made. We take pride in creating

sustainable product that come from

 the Earth and go back to the Earth.
