Low-carbon Design

The ingredients of Teatergent are:

79% tea seed powder, 15% baking

soda and 6% citric acid. All of them

are certified food-grade materials.

Food-grade

materials

Compressed

into cubes

By compressing the powder into tiny

solid cubes, we remove a lot of vacant

space and reduce the volume by 48%.

Recycled

tinplate box

The cubes are packed and protected

by recycled tinplate box. It can be

recycled over and over in the future

without damaging the environment.

Powerful Detergency

Tea makes us feel refreshing after having

a greasy meal, thanks to “tea saponin”.

Now, we amplify this effect by 130 times

to decompose and emulsify oil rapidly.

Before decompose:

Oil does not mix

with water.

After decompose:

Oil mixes with

water (emulsion).

High concentration “tea saponin” from

tea seed can easily decompose oil and

remove stubborn stuck-ons on the

surface of utensils and cookware, without

leaving invisible toxic chemical residue.
