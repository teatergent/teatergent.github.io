By compressing the powder into solid

cubes, we reduce the total volume by 48%

so we can put more in the same space.

The cubic form is great for space-filling

packaging. That’s how we reduce carbon

dioxide emission during transportation.

Recyclable & Sustainable

can be recycled repetitively without

causing any damage to the environment.

We manage to pack everything tightly, so
